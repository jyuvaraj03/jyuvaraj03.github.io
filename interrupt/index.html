Welcome to Interrupt 2018!

Interrupt is a National-Level Technical Symposium conducted by the students of the Computer Science department of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering. It is a place where engineers showcase their talent and ideas in a plethora of events across various domains of the computer world. Now in its 27th year, Interrupt is back and it's bigger and better than ever! Check out the Command Prompt for information on events and registration!
